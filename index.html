Welcome to Hard Surface Topology!

Here at Hard Surface Topology we aim to inform and guide emerging 3D artists in the world of hard surface, subdivision based 3D modelling. While newer tools like ZBrush's shadowbox, and other sculpting tools have come a long way in the world of hard surface modelling, we believe that handcrafted, subdivided meshes still offer the best results in a lot of cases, and are much more useful in applications where animation or performance are still key.

We're here to help you learn the fundamentals of subdivision modelling so that you'll have the basic tools you need to create any model you want with just a little bit of planning.
